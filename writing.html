THE WRITINGS OF
Matthew Gwynfryn Thomas

I write a column for SAPIENS magazine, called Machinations, about the human side of the 'artificial intelligence revolution' and technology.

A bunch of my published stories and poems (plus a film-poem) are on the Tangled Woof of Fact.

Other scribblings, mainly in the form of science journalism, have appeared in BioNews, SciDev.Net, the Wellcome Trust Blog, Shot of Science (now defunct) and Pi Media. Most of the published pieces are available via my Contently portfolio.

I don't really blog anymore but all the old entries are available on the Tangled Woof of Fact.
